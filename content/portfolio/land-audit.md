## Overview

Kirchhoff Surveyors needed to transition from CAD-based workflows to an enterprise GIS for auditing land assets. Afrispatial initially established the enterprise GIS, with Kartoza taking over to continue development. The project involved land under reservoirs, canals, pipelines, and water transfer schemes, requiring Professional Land Surveyor sign-off on reports.

![Land Audit](/img/portfolio/land-audit.png)

## Services Provided

Established enterprise GIS, trained staff, and built data capture and reporting tools. Data managed in PostGIS. Desktop users worked in QGIS with forms, styles, and workflows. Reporting with GeoServer and OpenLayers. Remote editing via WFS-T from GeoServer.

Developed the QGIS Surveyor General Diagram Downloader plugin.
