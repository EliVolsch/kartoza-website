Lindie is a Junior Consultant bringing fresh energy and formal GIS training to Kartoza's team. Her Diploma in Geo-Information and Technology from Esri College provided comprehensive grounding in geospatial concepts and technologies.

Based in South Africa, Lindie focuses on QGIS and coding automation, helping clients streamline their workflows and solve spatial problems efficiently. Her interest in automation reflects the growing importance of scripting and programming skills in modern GIS work.

Cartographic design is another area where Lindie excels. She understands that effective maps require both technical accuracy and aesthetic sensibility, and she works to achieve both in her projects.

As a Junior Consultant, Lindie works directly with clients to understand their needs and develop appropriate solutions. This role is helping her develop not only technical skills but also the communication and project management abilities essential for a successful GIS career.

Lindie represents the new generation of GIS professionals who are equally comfortable with traditional mapping techniques and modern programming approaches. Her combination of formal training and practical skills positions her well for continued growth in the field.
