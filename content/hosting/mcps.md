## Geospatial AI Made Simple

Model Context Protocol (MCP) endpoints bring the power of geospatial analysis to your AI applications. Whether you're building AI assistants, chatbots, or automated workflows, our hosted MCPs provide the spatial intelligence your applications need.

### What is MCP?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is an open standard that allows AI assistants to connect to external tools and data sources. Our hosted MCPs provide geospatial capabilities that AI models can use to answer location-based questions, perform spatial analysis, and work with geographic data.

### Use Cases

- **AI Chatbots**: Enable your chatbot to answer questions like "What's the nearest hospital?" or "Show me all parks within 5km"
- **Automated Workflows**: Build automation that processes addresses, calculates distances, or analyses spatial relationships
- **Data Enrichment**: Automatically geocode addresses or add administrative boundary information to your datasets
- **Field Applications**: Power mobile apps with routing, location search, and offline-capable spatial queries
