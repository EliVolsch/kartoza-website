## Overview

GEEST (Gender Enabling Environments Spatial Tool), formerly known as Geo3E, was built by Kartoza for The World Bank to implement the Geospatial Women's Employment Analytical Framework (GeoWEAF). The tool identifies location-specific factors that affect women's access to jobs, supporting evidence-based policy development.

## Key Features

The plugin analyses 15 spatially varying factors across three main dimensions:

### Contextual Factors
- Social norms and cultural factors
- Legal and regulatory environment
- Economic conditions

### Accessibility Factors
- Transport infrastructure
- Childcare facilities
- Healthcare access
- Financial services

### Place Characterisation
- Safety and security
- Urban/rural classification
- Infrastructure quality
- Employment centres proximity

## Use Cases

- Gender-responsive urban planning
- Infrastructure investment prioritisation
- Policy impact assessment
- Development project site selection
- SDG monitoring and reporting

## Installation

1. Open QGIS and go to **Plugins → Manage and Install Plugins**
2. Search for "GEEST"
3. Click **Install Plugin**
4. Access the analysis tools from your QGIS toolbar
