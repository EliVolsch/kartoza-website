Leon is the DevOps Manager at Kartoza, bringing over 20 years of IT industry experience to the role. His career has spanned development, systems administration, database administration, and solutions architecture - a breadth of experience that provides valuable perspective on infrastructure decisions.

His focus on DevOps since 2013 places him among the early adopters of this methodology. Leon understood early that breaking down silos between development and operations leads to better software and more efficient delivery.

As Technical Manager, Leon oversees Kartoza's infrastructure strategy and guides the DevOps team. His experience across multiple technical domains enables him to make informed decisions about technology choices and architectural patterns.

Leon's approach to DevOps emphasizes automation, monitoring, and reliability. He understands that infrastructure should enable rather than constrain development, and he works to ensure that Kartoza's systems provide a solid foundation for all projects.

Based in South Africa, Leon has watched the IT industry evolve through multiple paradigm shifts. This historical perspective, combined with his technical expertise, makes him well-equipped to guide Kartoza's infrastructure into the future.
