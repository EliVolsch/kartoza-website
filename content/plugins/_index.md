Kartoza develops and maintains a range of QGIS plugins that extend the capabilities of QGIS for specialised applications. From disaster risk assessment to biodiversity monitoring, our plugins help organisations unlock the full potential of open source GIS.

Our plugins are available through the official [QGIS Plugin Repository](https://plugins.qgis.org/) and are actively maintained by our team of QGIS experts.
